ü§ñ AI Setup Manual (Groq)

  1. Create Account: Go to console.groq.com and sign in.
  2. Generate Key: Click "API Keys" on the sidebar and create a new one.
  3. Copy Key: Copy the string starting with gsk_....
  4. Link AI: To link the AI, go to the repo and look for txt folder, after found, click token.txt and replace it with your token.
  5. You are done, you may need to rotate the keys after a while

üìä User Counter Manual (PubNub)

  1. Register: Visit PubNub.com and create a free account.
  2. App Instance: Create a new App in your dashboard titled "User Counter".
  3. Keyset: Create an Keyset, click into the app to find your Publish Key and Subscribe Key.
  4. Live feedback: to make it live feedback, go to keyrule for the keyset and enable all checkboxes, after that go to the tab Presence and enable "Generate Leave on TCP FIN or RST"
  5. You are done, you may need to rotate the keys after a while
